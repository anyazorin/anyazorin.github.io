Everyday in New York over 620,000 bike trips are made. Sitting by a bike lane and watching them all go by, you see not just the full spectrum of people, but also their chosen bikes. Bikes are the riders method of transport to a destination, to their next paycheck, to a new physical limit, and most of all to joy.
